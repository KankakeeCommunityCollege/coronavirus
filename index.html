Coronavirus (COVID-19) Response

There are no cases of Coronavirus at Kankakee Community College.

KCC is committed to reducing the potential spread of coronavirus (COVID-19). Following are statuses of KCC areas. Each status includes the last time it was updated. The newest status updates are shown in blue.

  • KCC-related cases of COVID-19 (coronavirus): None reported.
    (Updated 3/16/20, 9 a.m.)
  • College facilities: Open. Student support services like tutoring, advising, registration, food pantry, computer labs, and campus resources are still available. While on campus, please practice social distancing guidelines. The Centers for Disease Control defines social distancing as "remaining out of congregate settings, avoiding mass gatherings and maintaining distance (approximately 6 feet) from others when possible."
    (Unchanged since original post, 3/12/20, 4 p.m.)
  • Credit classes: All are canceled until March 23. Beginning on March 23 through April 20 faculty will teach lecture classes online. Each professor/instructor will communicate with their students regarding how their specific classes will proceed in an online environment. Some labs will continue to meet in person. Clinicals will continue as allowed by the host site.
    (Unchanged since original post, March 12, 4 p.m.)
  • Adult education: All classes are canceled until April 1.
    (Modified March 15, 7 p.m.)
  • Continuing education: KCC will contact you regarding course status.
    (Unchanged since original post, March 12, 4 p.m.)
  • Events sponsored by KCC: Events with 50 or more anticipated attendees are postponed at least through April 20. All gatherings should practice social distancing guidelines. Event organizers, including college sponsors of external groups, are responsible for informing guests and attendees that their event has been canceled. This includes outside organizers renting space at KCC, and KCC events at off-campus venues.
    (Unchanged since original post, March 12, 4 p.m.)
  • Fitness center: Open regular hours.
    (Unchanged since original post, March 12, 4 p.m.)
  • Athletic games and practices: Canceled until March 22.
    (Modified March 13, 1 p.m.)
  • Employees: Report to work March 16. Further information is available on my.kcc.edu in Update newsletter articles with FAQs and Dr. Michael Boyd’s message from March 12.
    (Modified March 14, 5 p.m.)
  • College-sponsored student travel: Suspended.
    (Modified March 13, 1 p.m. – to include athletics)
  • Everybody: Stay home if you are sick and follow established protocols to prevent the spread of viruses.
