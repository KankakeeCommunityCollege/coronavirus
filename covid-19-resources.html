COVID-19 resources

The college community is strongly encouraged to review information provided by the following:

  • The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
  • The U.S. Department of State advisories
  • The World Health Organization (WHO)
  • The Illinois Department of Public Health

Prevention and Treatment

Members of the college community are strongly encouraged to read the guidance provided by the CDC regarding preventive steps you can take to avoid the Coronavirus and the spread of respiratory diseases.
